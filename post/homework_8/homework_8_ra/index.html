Homework 8, research on application

Assignment:

  • Find in the web what are the distributions that you just simulated.

X

X has normal distribution generated with the Marsaglia method.

X^2

X^2 has Chi-squared distribution, which is defined as $$ Q = \sum_{i=1}^k Z^2_i $$

X/Y^2

X/Y^2 has Student-T distribution: $$ T_n = \frac{Z}{\sqrt{K/n}} $$

X^2/Y^2

X^2/Y^2 has Fisher-Snedecor distribution: $$ F = \frac{X/m}{Y/n}, $$ where X and Y both have Chi-squared distribution; m and n are the degrees of freedom

X/Y

X/Y has Cauchy distribution, its density function is $$ f_{(x_0,y_0)}(x) = \frac{y_0}{(x - x_0)^2 + y_0^2} $$
